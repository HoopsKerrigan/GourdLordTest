Purchase Stocks

To purchase stocks, enter your unique 8-digit login, enter the four-letter ticker of the stock you'd like to purchase, as well as the quantity of the stock you'd like to purchase.

Click "Review" to see the total cost of this purchase before clicking "Submit" to finalize the purchase. When the purchase is finalized, there is no way to undo the transaction.
